## Summary

While the formal theological tone is largely achieved, the prose contains minor tautological phrasing such as "Immediately as soon as."

## Findings

- [high] preserved-language-integrity: The source phrase 'Theologie der Thatsachen' (and 'Theologie der Rhetorik') was partially translated, resulting in 'Theology der Thatsachen'. The style guide requires preserving German spans exactly; the output corrupts the span by translating the cognate noun while leaving the genitive article and object in German.
